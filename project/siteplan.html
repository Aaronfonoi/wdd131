Website Project Plan - Car club

Project Subject

Subject: Car club

Description of Planned Content

  • car part reviews
  • Latest car news
  • Car guides and tips
  • Hardware recommendations
  • Community forum

Reason for Selection

I have a strong personal interest in all things cars and motor sports, and it's a widely popular topic with a large audience, making it ideal for creating engaging and valuable content.

Site Name

Example: Motor wrench car club

This name represents a comprehensive resource for PC gamers, covering reviews, news, tips, and a community forum.

Optional domain availability: motorwrench.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a place for car enthusiasts to connect and find help from others in the community.

Scenarios

  • Which parts are best if I want to make my car look better?
  • Where can I find tips to work on my car?
